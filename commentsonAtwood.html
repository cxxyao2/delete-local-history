Margaret Atwood has written many books throughout her career as an author. Some of her other notable works include: "Surfacing" (1972) "Lady Oracle" (1976) "Cat's Eye" (1988) "The Robber Bride" (1993) "Alias Grace" (1996) "The Blind Assassin" (2000) "Oryx and Crake" (2003) "The Year of the Flood" (2009) "MaddAddam" (2013) "The Heart Goes Last" (2015) These are just a few examples of Margaret Atwood's many works. She is a prolific and versatile writer who has produced an extensive body of work across multiple genres, including poetry, fiction, and non-fiction. Whether you're a fan of dystopian fiction, feminist literature, or simply great storytelling, Margaret Atwood's books are definitely worth checking out.
